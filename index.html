mailsloth

For when you can't be fucked with mailchimp.

Capture emails in a few steps.


1. Swap your email address for an api key.

We'll send a copy to your email address.


We'll never share your email with anyone else. Sending... Success! Check below.

2. Copy the script snippet onto your site.

<script src='//api.mailsloth.net/sloth.js'></script>

3. Configure your form element.

Choose from:

Copy and paste the following html snippet onto your site:


Create a form if you dont already have one and add the following to your email input element:

  • The class mailsloth-email-input
  • The attribute data-bind='' with your public key

The form will be modified to submit to the mailsloth servers before doing its default action.

See demo forms here.


4. Download your email list.
